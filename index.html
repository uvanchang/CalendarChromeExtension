UCSD Class Scheduler

What does this extension do?

This extension adds a button to your webreg page in order to easily add classes (enrolled and waitlisted) to your Google calendar from Week 1-10.

How to use it

First make sure to sign into the Google account that you want your classes added to in the upper right corner of this page. Then navigate to https://act.ucsd.edu/webreg2/start, and log into your UCSD account. An "Add to Google Calendar" button should appear here.

Enter the Monday of Week 1 date for the quarter you want to add in the format ##/##/#### (month/day/year), and it will add your classes to your primary calendar!
